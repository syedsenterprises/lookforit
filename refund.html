Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Refund Policy applies to all paid services provided by Lookforit.xyz, including tool listing fees, featured placement, and any sponsored content arrangements. Please read this policy carefully before completing a payment.

1. Paid Listing Services

Lookforit.xyz currently offers two types of paid tool listings:

  • Standard Listing ($49): A reviewed, editorial listing entry in the AI tools directory.
  • Featured Listing ($129): Priority placement with enhanced visibility and a featured badge.

These are one-time fees for the submission and review process. Payment covers the editorial review, verification, and publication work carried out by our team.

2. Refunds for Listing Submissions

2.1 Before Review

If you have paid for a listing submission and we have not yet begun the review process, you may request a full refund within 48 hours of payment. To request a refund, contact us at syedsinterprises@gmail.com with your submission details and payment confirmation.

2.2 After Review Has Begun

Once our editorial team has begun reviewing your submission, the listing fee becomes non-refundable. This is because the fee covers the time and work involved in reviewing, verifying, and writing the listing entry - which begins immediately upon receiving your submission.

2.3 If Your Listing Is Declined

In cases where Lookforit.xyz declines to publish a submitted tool (for example, because it violates our editorial standards, is not a genuine AI product, or contains inaccurate information), we will provide a full refund within 5-7 business days. We will notify you by email if your submission is declined along with the reason.

3. Featured Listing Cancellation

Featured listings are generally published within 5 business days of approved submission. If you wish to cancel or change your listing type after payment but before publication:

  • Downgrade from Featured to Standard is possible within 24 hours of payment - you will receive a $80 partial refund.
  • Cancellation within 24 hours of payment (before review begins) qualifies for a full refund.
  • After 24 hours or once review begins, the fee is non-refundable.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us via email at syedsinterprises@gmail.com with:

  • Your full name
  • The tool name you submitted
  • Your payment confirmation or reference number
  • The reason for your refund request

We will respond within 2-3 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days depending on your payment method.

5. Chargebacks

We ask that you contact us directly before initiating a chargeback with your bank or payment provider. Initiating an unjustified chargeback may result in your submission being removed and future submissions being declined. We are committed to resolving disputes fairly and promptly.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our paid services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact

For any questions about refunds, payments, or listing services, please reach out to us:

  • syedsinterprises@gmail.com

Or use our Contact form.
