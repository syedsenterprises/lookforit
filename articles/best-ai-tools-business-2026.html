Customer Support and Service Workflows

  • ChatGPT for response drafting, macro templates, and support knowledge workflows.
  • Claude for policy-safe long-form support responses and structured explanations.
  • Perplexity for source-cited troubleshooting research and fast internal investigation.

Marketing and Content Teams

  • Jasper for campaign copy and marketing content operations.
  • Canva AI for fast creative production and brand-safe visual iteration.
  • InVideo AI for script-to-video campaign outputs.
  • Grammarly for tone and clarity consistency across communication channels.

Sales and Revenue Enablement

  • Notion AI for call notes, summaries, and shared deal intelligence.
  • Fireflies.ai for meeting transcription and action extraction.
  • Otter.ai for searchable conversation history and recap workflows.

Operations and Internal Productivity

  • Make.com for workflow automation across apps and repetitive operations.
  • ClickUp AI for project execution support and team planning.
  • Airtable AI for data classification, summaries, and operational systems.
  • Coda AI for ops docs, decision tracking, and structured team workflows.

Technical and Product Teams

  • GitHub Copilot for implementation speed and coding support.
  • Cursor for AI-first development loops with project context.
  • LangChain for building internal AI-enabled product features.
  • LangSmith for debugging and observability in AI application workflows.

How to Roll Out AI Without Chaos

Adopt one workflow at a time, define a baseline, then track measurable improvement. Good rollout metrics include cycle time, output quality, and reduction in repetitive manual effort. Standardize only after a successful pilot.
