Study Planning and Notes

  • Notion AI for structured class notes and study dashboards.
  • Coda AI for organizing assignments and semester workflows.
  • ClickUp AI for task tracking and deadline planning.

Writing and Assignments

  • ChatGPT for brainstorming, outlines, and draft refinement.
  • Claude for long-form writing and structured summaries.
  • Grammarly for grammar, tone, and clarity improvements.
  • QuillBot for paraphrasing and rewrite support.

Research and Concept Understanding

  • Perplexity for source-backed research and quick concept checks.
  • Google Gemini for multimodal explanations and learning workflows.
  • Poe for multi-model comparisons when concepts are unclear.

Coding and Technical Learning

  • Codeium for coding assistance while learning to build.
  • GitHub Copilot for guided implementation in project-based learning.
  • Phind for technical debugging and explanation workflows.

How Students Should Use AI Responsibly

Use AI to understand, not to bypass learning. Ask for explanations, examples, and quiz prompts. Rewrite in your own words and validate facts using primary sources and class materials.
