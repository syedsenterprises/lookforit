About the Site

What is Lookforit.xyz?

Lookforit.xyz is a curated directory and resource hub for AI tools, useful websites, and online resources. Our goal is to save you hours of research by organising the best tools and guides in one consistently structured directory. Whether you're a developer, creator, student, or entrepreneur — there's something here for you.

Who runs Lookforit.xyz?

Lookforit.xyz is independently run by Shahid. The site is not affiliated with any AI company or tool provider listed in the directory. Our goal is to provide honest, unbiased descriptions of each tool based on publicly available information.

How often is the directory updated?

We aim to update tool entries when significant changes occur — such as new pricing, major feature launches, or product shutdowns. New tools are added on a rolling basis. The site is not updated on a fixed schedule but is reviewed regularly.

Does Lookforit.xyz endorse the tools listed?

Listing a tool does not constitute an official endorsement. We aim to describe tools accurately based on available information, but we encourage you to do your own research and trial before committing to any paid tool. Read our Disclaimer for more details.

Using the Directory

Is Lookforit.xyz free to use?

Yes, browsing the directory is completely free. You never need to create an account or pay anything to access tool listings, articles, or any other content on this site. Our revenue comes from paid tool submissions and advertising, not from readers.

How do I find a specific type of AI tool?

Use the search bar at the top of the sidebar to search by keyword. You can also browse by category — click AI Tools in the menu and use the category filter, or go directly to a category page from the homepage.

The tool I'm looking for isn't listed. What should I do?

Either contact us to suggest the tool, or if you are the tool's developer, submit it through our Submit Your Tool page. We review all suggestions.

Are tool descriptions written by AI?

No. All editorial descriptions on Lookforit.xyz are written or carefully reviewed by a human editor. We may use AI tools to assist with drafting, but every listing is checked for accuracy before publication.

Tool Listings & Submissions

How do I get my AI tool listed?

Submit your tool through our Submit Your Tool page. Fill in the details, choose your plan, and we'll review your submission. Standard listings start at $49 and Featured listings at $129.

What's the difference between Standard and Featured listings?

A Standard listing ($49) gives your tool a reviewed entry in the directory with a description, link, and relevant category tags. A Featured listing ($129) includes priority placement on category pages, a featured badge, and a more prominent position in the directory and homepage. Both are reviewed before publication.

How long does the review take?

Standard listings are typically reviewed within 5-7 business days. Featured listings are prioritised and usually reviewed within 3-5 business days. We will notify you by email once your listing is live or if additional information is required.

Can I update my listing after it's published?

Yes. If your tool updates its pricing, features, or name, contact us with the updated details. Minor corrections are made for free. Major rewrites may require a review fee.

What types of tools are not accepted?

We do not accept tools that are deceptive, illegal, or violate ethical guidelines. This includes tools designed to generate spam, mislead users, scrape data without consent, or engage in harmful activities. We also reserve the right to decline any submission without explanation.

Payments and Refunds

What payment methods are accepted?

We currently accept payments via major credit cards and PayPal through our submission form. If you have specific payment requirements, please contact us before submitting.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available within 48 hours of payment if the review has not yet begun. Once review starts or if a listing is approved, the fee is non-refundable. Declined submissions are refunded in full. Read our full Refund Policy for details.

I paid but haven't received any confirmation. What should I do?

Check your spam or junk folder first. If you still can't find a confirmation email, contact us at syedsinterprises@gmail.com with your payment details and we'll look into it promptly.

Technical Questions

The site isn't loading correctly on my phone. What should I do?

Lookforit.xyz is fully mobile-responsive. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, please let us know with details about your device and browser and we'll investigate.

How do I report a broken link or incorrect information?

We appreciate reports of inaccuracies or broken links. Please use our Contact page to let us know the page URL and the issue. We aim to fix reported errors within 48 hours.
